# mintCIO

TechCircle (Mint CIO) design system and page builds for HT Digital.

## Contents

- `techcircle-design/` — Claude skill source: SKILL.md, design tokens (`references/tokens.css`, `references/tokens.json`), and component patterns (`references/components.md`), extracted from TechCircle homepage builds v6.3 and v5.7.
- `techcircle-design.skill` — packaged skill, installable in Claude Cowork.
- `techcircle-article-f1.html` — article page (F1 IT director feature, adapted from CIO.com) in TechCircle style.
- `techcircle-section-ai.html` — Artificial Intelligence section page modeled on cio.com/artificial-intelligence, in TechCircle style.

## Design system at a glance

White surface, sharp corners (radius 0), Young Serif headings, Inter body, IBM Plex Mono meta/badges, Poppins nav, orange #f99d1c accent bar, blue #0069ff interaction color, black header with grey mono secondary nav.
